Our Philosophy

Cooked with
intention

We believe great food is never rushed. At Ember & Salt, every preparation begins the day before — slow brines, overnight rests, wood-smoked stocks.

Our chefs source from within 200km of Mumbai, working directly with farmers, fishermen, and foragers who share our obsession with quality.
